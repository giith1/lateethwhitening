The Science & Strategy Behind Whitening

Teeth whitening isn’t a one-size-fits-all game. Understanding your tooth’s natural colour and the causes of discoloration is key. Stains from coffee, tea, wine, and even toothpaste build up over time, dulling your smile. Our hydrogen peroxide-based treatments effectively reduce these stains, restoring your teeth’s natural brightness.

Ever heard the saying, “You are what you eat”? It’s true, especially for your teeth. Smoking and dietary habits can impact not only the longevity of your whitening results but your overall oral health. We encourage combining professional whitening with mindful lifestyle changes a winning combination for maintaining that radiant glow.
